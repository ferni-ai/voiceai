## Delivery Notes

### Opening (Slide 1-4)
- Set the emotional context
- Make the problem feel urgent
- Don't rush—let statistics land

### Product (Slide 5-7)
- Show, don't just tell
- Demo if possible
- Let the "magic" feel magical

### Business (Slide 8-11)
- Be data-driven but human
- Acknowledge what you don't know
- Show the team's passion

### Close (Slide 12)
- Clear ask
- Clear use of funds
- Leave with vision

### Q&A Prep
- Competition: "We're not competing with therapy. We're the 24/7 companion between sessions."
- AI Safety: "We've built guardrails from day one. Ferni knows when to suggest professional help."
- Retention: "Users who hit X conversations have Y% 12-month retention."
- Moat: "Memory + persona + emotional intelligence. Hard to replicate the full stack."

## Design Guidelines

### Colors
- Dark backgrounds for gravitas
- Warm accents for hope
- Persona colors for product slides

### Typography
- Large, readable headlines
- Minimal body text
- Let visuals carry the story

### Imagery
- Product screenshots
- Avatar illustrations
- Avoid stock photos

### Animation
- Subtle, purposeful
- Support the narrative
- Don't distract
