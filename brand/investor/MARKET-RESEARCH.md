## 1. Mental Health Services Market

### Market Size & Growth

| Metric | Value | Source Period |
|--------|-------|---------------|
| **2024 Market Size** | $448.0 - $451.1B | Global |
| **2032-2033 Projection** | $573.8 - $623.1B | 8-9 year forecast |
| **CAGR** | 3.0% - 4.3% | Steady, reliable growth |

### Key Drivers

1. **Destigmatization** — Mental health conversations becoming mainstream, especially post-pandemic
2. **Insurance Coverage Expansion** — More payers covering mental health services
3. **Workforce Mental Health** — Corporate wellness programs increasing demand
4. **Youth Mental Health Crisis** — Gen Z reporting highest anxiety/depression rates ever recorded

### Market Segments

| Segment | Characteristics |
|---------|-----------------|
| **Outpatient Services** | Largest segment, growing accessibility |
| **Inpatient Facilities** | Higher cost, more severe cases |
| **Digital Therapeutics** | Fastest growing, includes apps |
| **Teletherapy** | Post-COVID acceleration, sustained demand |

### Barrier to Entry for Traditional Therapy

- **Cost:** $150-300 per session (US average)
- **Waitlists:** 6-12 weeks for new patients common
- **Availability:** Only during business hours
- **Geographic:** Limited in rural areas

**Ferni Opportunity:** Fill the gaps between therapy sessions — the 167 hours per week when a therapist isn't available.
