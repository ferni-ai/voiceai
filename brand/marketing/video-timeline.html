Production Notes

Voice Casting

  • Gender: Neutral/flexible
  • Age: 30s-40s (mature but not old)
  • Tone: Warm, genuine, not announcer-y
  • Pace: Slightly slower than normal

Music Direction

  • Style: Ambient, emotional
  • Instruments: Piano, soft synths
  • Reference: Nils Frahm, Olafur Arnalds
  • Avoid: Corporate stock music

Animation Style

  • Type: 2D motion graphics
  • Feel: Organic, breathing, alive
  • Colors: Sage, warm earth tones
  • Avoid: Flat corporate graphics

Delivery Formats

  • YouTube: 16:9, 60s full
  • Instagram: 1:1, 60s reformat
  • TikTok: 9:16, 30s condensed
  • Website: 16:9, 60s hero
