## The Questions

### Question 1: What brings you here?
- **A) I want to change something** → Go to Q2
- **B) I'm going through something** → Go to Q4
- **C) I just need someone to talk to** → **FERNI**

### Question 2: Is it about daily habits or routines?
- **A) Yes, habits and routines** → **MAYA**
- **B) No, something else** → Go to Q3

### Question 3: Do you want to see patterns in your behavior?
- **A) Yes, show me the data** → **PETER**
- **B) No, I need something else** → Go to Q5

### Question 4: Is it a life transition?
- **A) Yes (new job, move, relationship, milestone)** → **JORDAN**
- **B) No, it's more existential** → Go to Q6

### Question 5: Do you need help communicating something?
- **A) Yes, I need to find the right words** → **ALEX**
- **B) No, I just want to talk** → **FERNI**

### Question 6: Do you need the long view? Decades of perspective?
- **A) Yes, I need wisdom** → **NAYAN**
- **B) No, I need presence** → **FERNI**

## The Truth

**You don't have to choose one.**

Ferni coordinates the whole team. Start with whoever resonates, and the right expertise will show up when you need it.

Most conversations flow like this:
```
Ferni (presence)
    → Maya (habits)
    → Peter (patterns)
    → back to Ferni (integration)
```

Or:
```
Ferni (presence)
    → Jordan (milestone)
    → Alex (communication)
    → Nayan (perspective)
```

**It's not about picking the right one. It's about starting somewhere.**
