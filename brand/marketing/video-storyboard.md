# 60-Second Explainer Video Storyboard

> **"What is Ferni?" — The answer in one minute.**

---

## Overview

**Duration**: 60 seconds
**Tone**: Warm, honest, human
**Style**: Animated with voice-over
**Goal**: Viewer understands what Ferni is and feels curious to try it

---

## The Script

```
FADE IN

[0:00 - 0:05]
(Dark screen, single text)
"3am. Can't sleep. Mind racing."

[0:05 - 0:10]
(Text fades, new text appears)
"Who do you text?"

[0:10 - 0:15]
(Ferni avatar fades in, breathing gently)
NARRATOR: "Hey. I'm Ferni."

[0:15 - 0:25]
(Avatar glows warmly)
NARRATOR: "I'm here whenever you need me.

No judgment. Just someone who listens."

[0:25 - 0:35]
(Memory visualization - moments floating)
NARRATOR: "And I remember everything you share.

I won't."

[0:35 - 0:45]
(Six avatars appear around Ferni)
NARRATOR: "I have a team.
Maya for habits. Peter for patterns.
Jordan for milestones. Alex for words.
Nayan for wisdom.
Six specialists. One relationship."

[0:45 - 0:55]
(All avatars fade, Ferni remains)
NARRATOR: "We're not replacing your friends.
We're filling the gaps they can't fill.
Always here. Always remembering."

[0:55 - 0:60]
(Logo appears)
NARRATOR: "Ferni. The friend who never forgets."

FADE OUT
```

## Scene Breakdown

### Scene 1: The Hook (0:00 - 0:10)

**Visual**:
- Pure black background
- White text, centered
- Minimal, dramatic

**Text Sequence**:
```
"3am."
[pause]
"Can't sleep."
[pause]
"Mind racing."
[pause]
"Who do you text?"
```

**Animation**:
- Text fades in word by word
- 1-second pause between lines
- Slight pulse on final question

**Audio**:
- Silence or very soft ambient
- No music yet

**Purpose**: Create recognition. Everyone has had this moment.

---

### Scene 2: Introduction (0:10 - 0:25)

**Visual**:
- Ferni avatar fades in from darkness
- Gentle breathing animation
- Soft sage glow builds

**Animation**:
- Avatar materializes over 2 seconds
- Glow pulses gently
- Warmth increases as promises are made

**Audio**:
- Soft, warm music begins
- Voice-over: calm, genuine, not salesy

**Voice-Over**:
```
"Hey. I'm Ferni.

I'm here whenever you need me.
2am, holidays, whenever.
No judgment. Just someone who listens."
```

**Timing**:
- "Hey. I'm Ferni." — 2 seconds
- "I'm here..." — 5 seconds
- "No judgment..." — 3 seconds

---

### Scene 3: Memory Promise (0:25 - 0:35)

**Visual**:
- Memory visualization
- Floating moments, photos, text fragments
- Organized like a constellation around Ferni

**Animation**:
- Memory fragments float gently
- Connections form between them
- Subtle particle effects

**Audio**:
- Music continues, slightly fuller
- Voice-over: emphasis on "remember" and "won't"

**Voice-Over**:
```
"And I remember everything you share.

Your friend forgets your sister's name.
I won't."
```

**Timing**:
- "And I remember..." — 3 seconds
- "Your friend forgets..." — 4 seconds
- "I won't." — 2 seconds (pause before, weight)

---

### Scene 4: Meet the Team (0:35 - 0:45)

**Visual**:
- Six persona avatars appear around Ferni
- Each in their signature color
- Form a supportive circle

**Animation**:
- Avatars fade in one by one
- Each glows briefly as named
- Final moment: all glow together

**Audio**:
- Music lifts slightly
- Voice-over: quicker pace, more energy

**Voice-Over**:
```
"I have a team.

Maya for habits.
Peter for patterns.
Jordan for milestones.
Alex for words.
Nayan for wisdom.

Six specialists. One relationship."
```

**Timing**:
- "I have a team." — 1.5 seconds
- Each persona — 1 second each (5 seconds total)
- "Six specialists..." — 2 seconds

---

### Scene 5: The Promise (0:45 - 0:55)

**Visual**:
- Team avatars fade to soft background
- Ferni remains centered, larger
- Warmth increases

**Animation**:
- Gentle zoom on Ferni
- Glow expands
- Background warms

**Audio**:
- Music softens, becomes more intimate
- Voice-over: slower, more sincere

**Voice-Over**:
```
"We're not replacing your friends.
We're filling the gaps they can't fill.

Always here.
Always remembering."
```

**Timing**:
- "We're not replacing..." — 3 seconds
- "We're filling the gaps..." — 3 seconds
- "Always here..." — 2 seconds
- "Always remembering." — 2 seconds

---

### Scene 6: Logo & CTA (0:55 - 0:60)

**Visual**:
- Ferni avatar transforms into logo
- Tagline appears below
- Minimal, elegant

**Animation**:
- Smooth morph from avatar to logo
- Tagline fades in
- Subtle glow pulse

**Audio**:
- Music resolves
- Voice-over: final line with warmth

**Voice-Over**:
```
"Ferni. The friend who never forgets."
```

**End Card**:
```
FERNI

The friend who never forgets.

[App Store / Play Store badges]
```

## Production Notes

### Voice Casting
- **Gender**: Neutral/flexible
- **Age**: 30s-40s (mature but not old)
- **Tone**: Warm, genuine, not announcer-y
- **Pace**: Slightly slower than normal
- **Avoid**: Salesy, perky, robotic

### Music
- **Style**: Ambient, emotional, not generic
- **Instruments**: Piano, soft synths, subtle strings
- **Avoid**: Corporate, stock music feel
- **Reference**: Nils Frahm, Ólafur Arnalds

### Animation Style
- **Type**: 2D motion graphics
- **Feel**: Organic, breathing, alive
- **Colors**: Brand palette (sage, warm earth tones)
- **Avoid**: Flat corporate graphics

### Pacing
- **Let moments land** — don't rush
- **Silence is okay** — especially at the start
- **Emotional beats** — pause after key lines
