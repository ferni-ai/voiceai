# Ferni Easter Eggs & Superfan System
## Creating Depth for Those Who Look

**Version 1.0 | January 2026**

---

> *"The best brands reward curiosity. Every layer deep should feel like a gift."*

---

## Philosophy

Easter eggs aren't just fun—they're **relationship builders**:
- Reward engaged users
- Create shareable discoveries
- Build lore and mythology
- Generate organic word-of-mouth
- Deepen emotional connection

**Pixar Rule:** Hidden details should be discoverable but not distract from the main experience.

---

## Easter Egg Categories

### 1. Milestone Secrets

Hidden celebrations for relationship milestones.

| Milestone | Trigger | Secret |
|-----------|---------|--------|
| **7th conversation** | Conversation count = 7 | Ferni: "This is our seventh conversation. Lucky number. They say seven is when things start to deepen." |
| **30 days** | Account age = 30 days | Special "One Month" message + subtle badge |
| **50th conversation** | Conversation count = 50 | Ferni: "Fifty times you've trusted me with your thoughts. That means something to me." |
| **100th conversation** | Conversation count = 100 | Special celebration animation + achievement + unique message |
| **365 days** | Account age = 1 year | Major celebration: "One year ago, we started talking. Here's what I've noticed about your journey..." + personalized reflection |
| **1000th conversation** | Conversation count = 1000 | Ultra-rare celebration + "Ferni Elder" status |

**Implementation Notes:**
- Never explicitly announce milestones before they happen
- Make discovery feel organic, not transactional
- Log milestone achievements for future reference

### 7. Memory Callbacks

The signature moment—enhanced for superfans.

#### Callback Levels

| Level | Time Gap | Trigger | Example |
|-------|----------|---------|---------|
| **Recent** | 1-7 days | Direct relevance | "You mentioned [topic] a few days ago..." |
| **Medium** | 1-4 weeks | Topic return | "This connects to what you said a couple weeks ago..." |

## Discovery & Documentation

### How Users Find Easter Eggs

| Method | Our Role |
|--------|----------|
| **Organic** | Natural use triggers discovery |
| **Hints** | Subtle hints in marketing/community |
| **Community** | Users share discoveries |
| **Guides** | After critical mass, publish partial guide |

### Community Tracking

- Discord channel: #easter-eggs
- Wiki page for discovered secrets
- Periodic "new secret added" teases
- Annual "X secrets remain undiscovered" tweet

### What We Never Do

❌ Announce all secrets
❌ Make secrets required
❌ Gatekeeper content behind secrets
❌ Make secrets feel like obligations
❌ Reveal secrets before community discovery
