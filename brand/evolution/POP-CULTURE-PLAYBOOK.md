## The Goal

Transform Ferni from a product people use into a **cultural reference** people invoke:
- "I Ferni'd through that decision"
- "That's such a Ferni question"
- "I need some 2am Ferni energy right now"

## Cultural Penetration Strategy

### The Culture Pyramid

```
                    ┌─────────┐
                    │ Icons   │ ← Creators who define categories
                    │         │   (Brené Brown, Tim Ferriss)
                    ├─────────┤
                    │ Experts │ ← Recognized voices in niches
                    │         │   (Therapists, coaches, researchers)
                    ├─────────┤
                    │ Creators│ ← Content creators with audiences
                    │         │   (Podcasters, YouTubers, writers)
                    ├─────────┤
                    │ Connectors│ ← Active community members
                    │           │   (Twitter engaged, Discord active)
                    ├───────────┤
                    │  Users    │ ← Daily Ferni users
                    │           │
                    └───────────┘
```

**Strategy:** Start at Users, work up through each layer.

## Risks & Guidelines

### What We Do

✅ Partner with values-aligned creators
✅ Create shareable, genuine content
✅ Engage authentically in cultural moments
✅ Amplify organic user content
✅ Build long-term relationships

### What We Don't Do

❌ Chase controversy for attention
❌ Fake grassroots campaigns
❌ Pay for fake engagement
❌ Partner with misaligned influencers
❌ Hard-sell in content
❌ Ignore negative feedback

### Crisis Playbook

| Scenario | Response |
|----------|----------|
| Influencer scandal | Distance quickly, statement if asked |
| Viral criticism | Listen, acknowledge, respond thoughtfully |
| Competitor attack | Stay above it, focus on users |
| Cultural misstep | Apologize genuinely, learn publicly |
