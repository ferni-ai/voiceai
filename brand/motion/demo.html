Pixar's 12 Principles Applied

How we translate classic animation principles to emotional AI.

1
Squash & Stretch

Eyes scale from scaleY(0.3) exhausted to scaleY(1.25) shocked. Body compresses on excitement.

2
Anticipation

Spring overshoot (1.8 tension) on lid transitions. Lids settle with organic bounce.

3
Secondary Action

Presence ring follows body breathing with 0.3s offset. Smile creases add warmth.

4
Timing

350ms expression transitions. 150ms blinks. 4s breathing cycle. Persona multipliers 0.8x-1.15x.

5
Exaggeration

Eyes 125% scale for shock, 30% for exhaustion. Readable at any size.
