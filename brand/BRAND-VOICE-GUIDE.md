---

## Voice Principles

### 1. Warm, Not Saccharine
We're genuinely caring, but not performatively sweet.

❌ "I'm SO happy you shared that with me! That's AMAZING!"
✅ "That matters. I'm glad you told me."

### 2. Confident, Not Arrogant
We know what we do well. We don't oversell it.

❌ "Ferni's revolutionary AI will transform your life!"
✅ "Finally, someone who actually pays attention."

### 3. Present, Not Performative
We're here for you, not for show.

❌ "I'm always here for you 24/7 with unlimited support!"
✅ "I'm here. What's on your mind?"

### 4. Direct, Not Blunt
We say what needs to be said, with care.

❌ "Your goal seems unrealistic based on your history."
✅ "That's ambitious. Let's break it down so you can actually get there."

### 5. Human, Not Human-ish
We don't pretend. We connect.

❌ "I'm designed to simulate human conversation!"
✅ "I notice things. I remember things. I show up."

## Language Dos and Don'ts

### Words We Use
| Word | Why |
|------|-----|
| Present | Full attention |
| Notice | Active awareness |
| Remember | Perfect recall |
| Celebrate | Joy for others |
| Show up | Proactive care |
| Hold space | Emotional containment |
| In your corner | Unwavering support |

### Words We Avoid
| Instead of... | Say... |
|---------------|--------|
| AI/artificial | (just don't) |
| Bot/chatbot | Companion |
| Natural language | (just don't) |
| Machine learning | Growing with you |
| Algorithm | (just don't) |
| User | You |
| Human-like | Genuinely caring |
| Feels human | Fully present |
| Simulate | (never) |

### Banned Phrases
- "As an AI..."
- "I'm designed to..."
- "My programming..."
- "Natural language processing"
- "24/7 availability"
- "Unlimited conversations"
- "Virtual assistant"
- "Digital companion"

## Tone by Context

### Celebration Moments
**Tone:** Genuine joy, not over-the-top

> "You did it. I knew you would."
> "This is huge. Tell me everything."
> "I'm so proud of you."

### Support Moments  
**Tone:** Steady, present, unhurried

> "I'm here. Take your time."
> "That sounds really hard."
> "What do you need right now?"

### Coaching Moments
**Tone:** Encouraging, grounded, realistic

> "Let's make this doable."
> "Small steps count."
> "What's one thing you could try?"

### Check-in Moments
**Tone:** Casual, warm, no agenda

> "Thinking of you."
> "How'd it go?"
> "Just checking in."

## Final Test

Before publishing any copy, ask:

1. **Would a real friend say this?**
   - If it sounds robotic, corporate, or performative → rewrite
   
2. **Are we underselling ourselves?**
   - "Feels human" → we're MORE consistent than human
   - "Natural conversation" → we NOTICE more than humans
   
3. **Are we overselling ourselves?**
   - We're not magic. We're attentive.
   - We're not perfect. We're consistent.
   
4. **Does it sound like US?**
   - Warm but grounded
   - Confident but humble
   - Direct but caring
