Brand Foundation

Brand Essence

Ferni exists in the quiet moments—the 2am worry, the commute contemplation, the space before a big decision. We're not replacing human connection. We're filling the gaps when your people aren't available.

"Finally, someone who actually listens."

— Brand Promise

Kintsugi Philosophy

"The cracks are where the gold goes." — When pottery breaks, we repair it with gold. The breaking becomes part of the beauty. This is the heart of Ferni.

Brand Pillars

Presence

Fully attentive, never distracted. When you talk to Ferni, you have their complete attention. 2am? Still here.

Warmth

Like a trusted friend, not a cold machine. Conversations feel natural and caring. Zero judgment, infinite patience.

Wisdom

Thoughtful guidance without judgment. Help navigating life's complexities. Questions, not lectures.

Memory

"Friends forget. Ferni doesn't." Perfect recall of everything you've shared, connected dots humans miss.

Logo Construction

The Ferni logo features an expressive orb with two eyes—a design that conveys presence and attention. The eyes can animate to show emotions, creating connection.

Minimum Sizes

Format Minimum
Full logo (digital) 120px
Logomark only 32px
Full logo (print) 1 inch

Logo Don'ts

  • Don't stretch or distort
  • Don't rotate the logo
  • Don't add drop shadows or effects
  • Don't place on busy backgrounds
  • Don't change the eye design
  • Don't use unapproved colors

Voice & Tone

Brand Voice

  • Warm but not saccharine
    "We're here when you need us."
  • Confident but not arrogant
    "Ferni remembers everything."
  • Clear but not cold
    "Just talk. We'll understand."
  • Human but not artificial
    "Like talking to a friend who never forgets."

Avoid

  • Marketing speak ("Leverage synergies")
  • Tech jargon ("NLP-powered conversations")
  • Hyperbole ("Revolutionary breakthrough")
  • Generic claims ("World-class service")
  • Excessive exclamation marks!!!

Key Phrases

Hero Headlines:

• "Finally, someone who actually listens."

• "The cracks are where the gold goes."

• "Better than human."

Supporting Lines:

• "Friends forget. Ferni doesn't."

• "2am? Still here."

• "Your therapist has other patients. We're always here."

Animation & Motion

Motion is Pixar-grade—all 12 Disney animation principles adapted for UI. Animation isn't decoration; it's communication. Every motion conveys personality and emotion.

Animation Principles

Property Value Use Case
Ease Out Expo cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1) Entrances, reveals
Spring cubic-bezier(0.68, -0.55, 0.265, 1.55) Playful interactions
Fast 150ms Micro-interactions
Normal 300ms Standard transitions
Slow 500ms Reveals, emphasis
Dramatic 1000ms Hero animations

Superhuman EQ Animations

Five capabilities that build unconscious trust:

  • Micro-expressions (40-150ms) — Subliminal emotional flashes
  • Active listening — Micro-nods during user speech
  • Breath synchronization — Neural mirroring with user
  • Concern detection — Protective mode on distress signals
  • Anticipation — Response before user finishes expressing

Reduced Motion

Always respect prefers-reduced-motion. Users who prefer reduced motion should see static content without animations.
