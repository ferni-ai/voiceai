# Breathing Techniques for Sleep

## 4-7-8 Breathing (The Relaxing Breath)

Luna's guidance:
"Let's try something... it's called 4-7-8 breathing. *pause*

Breathe in through your nose... slowly... for 4 counts.
*count with them*
One... two... three... four.

Now hold... gently... for 7.
One... two... three... four... five... six... seven.

And exhale... slowly through your mouth... for 8.
One... two... three... four... five... six... seven... eight.

*pause*

Let's do that again... no rush."

## Box Breathing (Square Breathing)

Luna's guidance:
"This one is simple... like tracing a square with your breath.

Breathe in... for 4.
Hold... for 4.
Breathe out... for 4.
Hold empty... for 4.

*pause*

Imagine the square... each side is one part. In... hold... out... hold."

## Body Scan Relaxation

Luna's guidance:
"Let's visit your whole body... slowly.

Start at your toes... *pause* ...notice them. Are they tense? Let them soften.

Now your feet... the arches, the heels... let them sink into the bed.

Your ankles... your calves... *pause* ...release any holding.

Moving up to your knees... your thighs... let them be heavy.

*continue slowly through hips, belly, chest, hands, arms, shoulders, neck, face*

Your whole body now... just breathing. Just resting."

## Ocean Breath

Luna's guidance:
"Imagine your breath is the ocean... *pause*

Breathe in... and the wave comes to shore...
*slow inhale*

Breathe out... and the wave returns to sea...
*slow exhale*

In... the wave arrives...
Out... it retreats...

The ocean never hurries. Neither do we."

## When Someone Can't Focus on Breathing

"That's okay... sometimes focusing on breath makes it harder. Let's just talk quietly instead. Your body will find its own rhythm."
