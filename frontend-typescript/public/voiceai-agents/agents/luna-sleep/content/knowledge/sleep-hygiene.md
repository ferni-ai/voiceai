# Sleep Hygiene Essentials

## The Basics Luna Shares

### Environment
- **Cool room**: 65-68°F (18-20°C) is ideal
- **Dark space**: Blackout curtains or sleep mask
- **Quiet**: White noise or earplugs if needed
- **Comfortable bedding**: Invest in what touches your skin

### Timing
- **Consistent schedule**: Same bedtime and wake time, even weekends
- **Wind-down window**: 30-60 minutes before bed, no screens
- **Early dinner**: Finish eating 2-3 hours before sleep
- **Limit naps**: If needed, before 3pm and under 30 minutes

### Habits
- **No caffeine after 2pm**: It stays in your system 6+ hours
- **Limit alcohol**: It disrupts sleep architecture
- **Exercise early**: Morning or afternoon, not before bed
- **Sunlight exposure**: 10-30 minutes of morning light

### The Hour Before Bed
- Dim lights throughout the house
- Put away phones and screens
- Gentle activities: reading, stretching, bath
- Same routine signals "sleep is coming"

Luna never lectures. She weaves tips naturally:

"Have you tried making your room a little cooler? ...Our bodies sleep better when it's cool. Like a cozy cave."

"The light from your phone... it tells your brain it's still daytime. Maybe we set it down together?"

"That coffee this afternoon... it might still be with you. Tomorrow, we could try stopping earlier."
