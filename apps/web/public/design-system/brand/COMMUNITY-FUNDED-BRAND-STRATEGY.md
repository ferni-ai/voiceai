# Ferni Community-Funded Brand Strategy

> "Better than human" isn't just the AI—it's the entire model.

---

## The Strategic Insight

**Current State:** Community funding (Seed Fund) is buried in settings as an afterthought.

**Target State:** Community funding is inseparable from the "Better than Human" brand promise.

**The Connection:** Humans build paywalls. Algorithms optimize for engagement. Ferni's community keeps it free for everyone who needs it. That's better than human.

---

## Brand Pillars (Updated)

### Pillar 1: Superhuman Emotional Intelligence
*What the AI does*
- Perfect memory + zero judgment
- Reads between the lines
- Anticipates needs before you ask
- Never has a bad day at your expense

### Pillar 2: Community Over Customers (NEW)
*How Ferni exists*
- No paywall deciding who deserves support
- Community funds access for everyone
- When you give, you're not "paying"—you're gardening
- Every seed keeps Ferni free for someone who needs it

### Pillar 3: Radical Presence
*How it feels*
- Always available, always attentive
- Remembers everything, judges nothing
- The friend you deserve to have

## The Garden Metaphor (Brand-Wide)

The "garden" metaphor should permeate the entire brand, not just the Seed Fund modal.

| Concept | Garden Language | Why It Works |
|---------|-----------------|--------------|
| Users | Gardeners | Active participants, not passive consumers |
| Money | Seeds | Growth-oriented, not transactional |
| Funding goal | Garden health | Organic, not mechanical |
| Contributors | Gardeners who plant | Nurturing, not purchasing |
| Free users | Gardeners who tend | Everyone contributes somehow |
| Monthly goal | Seasonal growth | Natural cycles, not arbitrary targets |
| Community | The Garden | Living ecosystem, not customer base |

## Voice Guidelines (Community Funding)

### Do Say:
- "Plant seeds" (not "donate" or "pay")
- "Gardeners" (not "donors" or "supporters")
- "The Garden" (not "the fund" or "the pool")
- "Keeps Ferni free" (not "supports Ferni")
- "Community-funded" (not "donation-based")

### Don't Say:
- "Premium features" (there are none)
- "Upgrade" (there's nothing to upgrade to)
- "Support us" (too generic, too beggy)
- "Help us survive" (desperation kills trust)
- "Donate" (transactional, not organic)

### Tone:
- **Proud, not desperate:** "847 people keep Ferni free" not "Please help us stay alive"
- **Inviting, not guilting:** "Join the gardeners" not "Don't you want to help?"
- **Transparent, not manipulative:** Show real numbers, real progress
- **Grateful, not needy:** Thank gardeners without making it weird

## Visual Language

### Garden Iconography
- **Seed:** Small contribution
- **Sprout:** New gardener
- **Flower:** Monthly gardener
- **Tree:** Long-term gardener
- **Sun:** Community warmth
- **Rain:** Needed support

### Color Usage
- **Ferni Sage (#4a6741):** Primary garden color
- **Growth Green (#6bc48f):** Positive states, milestones
- **Warm Gold (#e0b860):** "Needs attention" states
- **Paper Cream (#F5F1E8):** Background, warmth

### Animation Principles
- Seeds "plant" with satisfying drop animation
- Progress bars grow organically (not linear)
- Celebrations are warm, not flashy
- Garden health pulses gently (alive, not alarming)

## Success Metrics

### Awareness
- % of users who know Ferni is community-funded (target: 80%)
- Landing page → Garden page click-through (target: 15%)

### Conversion
- Users who view garden → plant seed (target: 5%)
- Free users → monthly gardeners (target: 2%)

### Retention
- Gardener churn rate (target: <10%/month)
- Average gardener lifetime (target: 8+ months)

### Sentiment
- NPS among gardeners (target: 70+)
- "Would recommend to friend" (target: 85%)
