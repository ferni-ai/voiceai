Brand Foundation

Brand Essence

Ferni exists in the quiet moments—the 2am worry, the commute contemplation, the space before a big decision. We're not replacing human connection. We're filling the gaps.

"Finally, someone who actually listens."

— Brand Promise

Brand Pillars

Presence

Fully attentive, never distracted. When you talk to Ferni, you have their complete attention.

Warmth

Like a trusted friend, not a cold machine. Conversations feel natural and caring.

Wisdom

Thoughtful guidance without judgment. Help navigating life's complexities.

Grounding

Calm, stable, reliable presence. A steady voice when life feels chaotic.

Design Philosophy

Our visual language draws from Japanese Zen aesthetics (clean, uncluttered, purposeful), Scandinavian Hygge (warm, inviting, human), and Apple precision (premium, polished, considered).

Logo Construction

The Ferni logo consists of two elements: the "FE" monogram in a rounded square, and the "Ferni" wordmark in Plus Jakarta Sans Bold.

Minimum Sizes

Format Minimum
Full logo (digital) 120px
Logomark only 32px
Full logo (print) 1 inch

Logo Don'ts

  • Don't stretch or distort
  • Don't rotate the logo
  • Don't add drop shadows or effects
  • Don't place on busy backgrounds
  • Don't change the logomark shape
  • Don't use unapproved colors

Voice & Tone

Brand Voice

  • Warm but not saccharine
    "We're here when you need us."
  • Confident but not arrogant
    "Ferni remembers everything."
  • Clear but not cold
    "Just talk. We'll understand."
  • Human but not artificial
    "Like talking to a friend who never forgets."

Avoid

  • Marketing speak ("Leverage synergies")
  • Tech jargon ("NLP-powered conversations")
  • Hyperbole ("Revolutionary breakthrough")
  • Generic claims ("World-class service")
  • Excessive exclamation marks!!!
  • Emojis in professional contexts

Headline Examples

Hero Headlines:

• "Finally, someone who actually listens."

• "Your AI team. Always there."

• "The space between."

Section Headlines:

• "They remember. So you don't have to."

• "A team that actually knows you."

• "Fast enough to feel like thinking."

Photography & Motion

Photography Style

  • Warm: Golden hour, amber tones
  • Natural: Real people, real settings
  • Intimate: Close, personal, connected
  • Grounded: Earth tones, natural materials

Color Treatment

Warm color grading, lifted shadows, natural saturation, subtle film grain, Paper Cream highlight bias.

Don'ts

  • Stock photo aesthetic
  • Corporate/sterile settings
  • Cool blue color casts
  • Harsh, artificial lighting
  • Posed, fake expressions
  • Neon or saturated colors

Animation Principles

Property Value Use Case
Ease Out cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1) Entrances, reveals
Fast 150ms Micro-interactions
Normal 300ms Standard transitions
Slow 500ms Reveals, emphasis
Cinematic 800ms+ Hero animations

Reduced Motion

Always respect prefers-reduced-motion. Users who prefer reduced motion should see static content without animations.
