Adaptive Design Philosophy

watchOS (Glanceable)

Single-stat focus. Circular progress rings. Essential insight only. No scrolling. 2-second comprehension goal.

iPhone (Thumb-Friendly)

Vertical card stack. Progress bars. Quick actions reachable by thumb. Swipeable insights.

Android (Material You)

Similar to iOS but with Material Design 3 styling. Dynamic color theming. Bottom sheet navigation.

Web (Full Experience)

Multi-column layouts. Rich visualizations. SVG charts. Hover interactions. Full narrative context.

iPad (Spatial Canvas)

Multi-column grid. Side-by-side comparisons. Apple Pencil hover states. Landscape-first orientation.

Apple TV (10-Foot UI)

Focus-based navigation. Large typography. Ambient mode. Siri Remote optimized. Living room viewing distance.

CarPlay (Driver-Safe)

Minimal interaction. Large touch targets. High contrast. Voice-first. Glanceable at 1 second. No complex visuals.

Widgets (At-a-Glance)

Small/Medium/Large sizes. Deep links only. No scrolling. Refreshed periodically. Timeline-based updates.
