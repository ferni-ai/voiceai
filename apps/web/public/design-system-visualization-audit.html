Design Philosophy

Metrics vs Meaning

Current App (Metrics-Focused)

Shows what happened with numbers and stats:

  • Progress percentages (67% complete)
  • Streak counters (14 days)
  • Accuracy scores (78% correct)
  • Total counts (42 conversations)
  • Trend indicators (↑ 12%)

Storytelling Showcase (Meaning-Focused)

Shows what it means with narrative:

  • Life Seasons (You're in a season of Growth)
  • Conversation River (Topics flowing through time)
  • The Mirror (What you say vs what I notice)
  • Ripple Effects (Better sleep cascaded into everything)
  • Unfinished Stories (Story arcs you started but haven't resolved)

"Apple and Google show you data. Ferni shows you yourself."

— The differentiating principle

Design Principles

Storytelling Standards

Every Visualization Must...

  • Tell a story, not just show data
  • Use human language ("You're in a season of Growth")
  • Show meaning before metrics
  • Connect to emotional journey
  • Support both Midnight and Zen themes

Never Show...

  • Raw numbers without context
  • Percentages without narrative
  • Charts that require interpretation
  • Data that doesn't serve growth
  • Metrics that could cause anxiety

"The goal isn't to show users more data. It's to help them feel truly known."

— Ferni Design Philosophy
