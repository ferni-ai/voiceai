STYLE GUIDE FOR ALL IMAGES:

LOCATION INSPIRATION:
- Park City, Utah - mountain town serenity
- Alpine meadows with wildflowers
- Golden aspen groves
- Wasatch Mountain backdrops
- Cozy mountain cabin warmth
- Historic Main Street charm

MOOD:
- Warm, human, grounding
- Contemplative but not sad
- Peaceful but not sterile
- Premium but not cold
- Mountain town tranquility

LIGHTING:
- Golden hour (warm amber)
- Natural, soft, directional
- Avoid harsh shadows
- Film photography aesthetic

COLOR GRADING:
- Warm undertones always
- Muted, natural saturation
- Earth tones dominant
- NO cool blue tints
- NO neon or bright colors

FEEL:
- Japanese zen meets Scandinavian hygge
- Apple sophistication meets Studio Ghibli heart
- Trusted friend, not corporate brand
- Handcrafted, not mass-produced

AVOID:
- Stock photo sterility
- Corporate/tech aesthetic
- Cool blue color casts
- Harsh, artificial lighting
- Posed, fake expressions
- Neon or saturated colors
- Sharp, cold geometry
