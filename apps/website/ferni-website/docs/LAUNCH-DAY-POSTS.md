### Behind the Scenes
```
Today we're launching Ferni, and I want to share something about how we built it.

We didn't start with AI capabilities.

We started with a question: What makes someone feel truly heard?

The answer led to some unusual decisions:

1. Voice-first design
Real conversations aren't typed. They flow. They have pauses. They have emotion. So we made Ferni voice-native, not voice-enabled.

2. Perfect memory
Every conversation builds on the last. No "remind me what we talked about." Your journey is continuous.

3. Zero prompts
You don't learn Ferni. Ferni learns you. Just talk naturally about whatever's on your mind.

4. Team, not tool
Six specialists who hand off seamlessly. Need research? Peter's already on it with full context.

The result feels less like AI and more like... support.

That's the goal. That's what we're launching today.

Try it free: ferni.ai

#BuildInPublic #AI #Startup
```
