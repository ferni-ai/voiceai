##  Color Grading Notes

Some images may need light color grading to match Ferni's warm palette:

```
Target Colors:
- Highlights: #F5F1E8 (Paper Cream)
- Midtones: Push toward warm amber
- Shadows: Warm browns, NOT cool grays

Adjustments:
- Temperature: +10-15 (warmer)
- Tint: +5 (toward magenta, away from green)
- Lift blacks slightly for softer shadows
```
