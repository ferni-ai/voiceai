## Brand Overview

### About Ferni

Ferni is an AI voice companion designed for meaningful connection—not productivity. Unlike typical AI assistants focused on tasks and transactions, Ferni provides a team of 6 AI specialists who remember you, understand context, and are always available when you need someone.

**Tagline:** "Better than human. Always available."

**Core Promise:** The connection of a supportive friend without the burden on real relationships.

## Typography

### Display Font: Plus Jakarta Sans

Used for headlines, titles, and display text.

- **Weights**: Bold (700), Extrabold (800)
- **Source**: [Google Fonts](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Plus+Jakarta+Sans)

### Body Font: Inter

Used for body text, UI elements, and captions.

- **Weights**: Regular (400), Medium (500), Semibold (600)
- **Source**: [Google Fonts](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Inter)

### Hierarchy

| Level | Font | Size | Weight |
|-------|------|------|--------|
| Display | Plus Jakarta Sans | 48-72px | Extrabold |

| Heading 2 | Plus Jakarta Sans | 24-32px | Bold |

## Usage Guidelines

### Do

- Use official logo files—don't recreate
- Maintain minimum clear space (1× logo height)
- Use persona colors correctly for each character
- Maintain LUXO-style eyes (opaque white, no pupils)
- Use approved background colors

### Don't

- Add pupils or alter avatar eyes
- Stretch or distort logos
- Use unapproved colors
- Place on busy backgrounds without sufficient contrast
- Modify the wordmark typography
