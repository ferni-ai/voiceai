## 🖼️ Hero/Lifestyle Photography Specs

### General Requirements
- **Aspect Ratios**: 16:9 (landscape), 4:5 (portrait), 1:1 (square)
- **Resolution**: Minimum 300 DPI for print, 72 DPI for web
- **Color Profile**: sRGB for web, Adobe RGB for print
- **File Size**: Optimize for web (< 500KB for fast loading)

### Style Guide
- Warm, golden hour lighting
- Natural, candid moments
- Diverse, authentic people
- Cozy, real environments (not sterile/corporate)
- Earth tones, sage green accents
- Film grain for warmth
- Shallow depth of field
