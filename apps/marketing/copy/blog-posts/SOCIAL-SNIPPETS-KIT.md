## Quick Reference: Voice Dos and Don'ts

### DO ✅

- Start with an observation or insight, not an announcement
- Use "we" and "you" — conversational, not corporate
- Be specific about challenges and tradeoffs
- Include something human (hesitation, surprise, what we learned)
- End with an invitation, not a demand

### DON'T ❌

- "We're excited to announce..." (corporate opener)
- "Revolutionary/groundbreaking/game-changing" (hyperbole)
- "Our proprietary technology leverages..." (jargon)
- All caps for emphasis (feels aggressive)
- More than 2 hashtags on Twitter, more than 10 on Instagram
