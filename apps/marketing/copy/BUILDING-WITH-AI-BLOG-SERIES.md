# Building Ferni: An AI-Native Company
## Blog Series Strategy & Content Plan

> "We're not just using AI to build a product—we're building a company where AI is a partner in everything we do."

---

## 📖 Series Overview

**Theme**: The authentic story of building an AI life coaching platform with AI as a development partner.

**Why This Works for Ferni**:
- Demonstrates depth and expertise (builds trust)
- Positions Ferni as thought leaders in AI development
- Humanizes the tech (aligns with brand: warm, grounded, human)
- Provides shareable, long-form content for LinkedIn/Twitter
- Attracts both users AND investors/press

**Brand Voice Reminders**:
- ✅ Warm but not saccharine
- ✅ Confident but not arrogant  
- ✅ Clear but not cold
- ✅ Human but not artificial
- ❌ No tech jargon or marketing speak
- ❌ No hyperbole ("revolutionary", "breakthrough")

## 📚 The Blog Series: "Building in Public"

### Post 2: The Architecture

## ✏️ Writing Guidelines

### Voice Checklist

Before publishing, verify:

- [ ] Would a friend say this? (warm, not corporate)
- [ ] Is it honest about limitations? (no overpromising)
- [ ] Does it teach something? (value for reader)
- [ ] Is the tech jargon explained or removed?
- [ ] Does it sound like Ferni? (grounded, wise, present)

### Structure

- Start with a hook (observation, question, or story)
- One idea per paragraph
- Use subheadings every 200-300 words
- Include at least one human moment (story, quote, example)
- End with reflection, not sales pitch
- CTA should feel like an invitation, not an ask

### Examples of Good Ferni Voice

> ✅ "We made a lot of mistakes building this. Here's the one that taught us the most."

> ✅ "Memory is tricky. Get it wrong, and you're creepy. Get it right, and you're a good friend."

> ✅ "Honestly? We're still figuring this out. But here's what we know so far."

### Examples of NOT Ferni Voice

> ❌ "Our revolutionary AI technology leverages cutting-edge NLP to deliver unprecedented results."

> ❌ "Ferni is the world's most advanced AI life coaching platform."

> ❌ "Sign up now to transform your life with AI! 🚀"
