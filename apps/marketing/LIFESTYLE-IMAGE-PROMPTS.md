# Ferni Lifestyle Photography Prompts
## Apple-Style Human-Centered Imagery

> These prompts are designed for AI image generation (Midjourney, DALL-E, Imagen, Veo) 
> or as creative briefs for photographers.

---

## 🎨 Style Foundation

### The Ferni Look
```
Warm, authentic, intimate. Golden hour warmth. Film grain texture. 
Shallow depth of field. Natural environments. Real moments, not posed.
Earth tones: sage green, warm cream, natural browns.
NOT: corporate, sterile, stock photo aesthetic, cool blues, neon.
```

### Technical Specs for All Prompts
Add to any prompt:
```
Shot on 35mm film, Kodak Portra 400, shallow depth of field, 
natural window light, warm color grading, subtle film grain, 
85mm lens, f/1.8 aperture, golden hour warmth
```

## 🚫 What to Avoid

### Visual Don'ts
- ❌ Stock photo poses (fake smiles, pointing at screens)
- ❌ Corporate environments (glass offices, conference rooms)
- ❌ Cool blue lighting
- ❌ Neon or tech-bro aesthetics
- ❌ Sterile, minimalist-to-a-fault backgrounds
- ❌ Overly polished/retouched skin
- ❌ Forced diversity (unnatural groupings)
- ❌ Direct eye contact with camera (feels salesy)

### Conceptual Don'ts
- ❌ AI visualizations or robots
- ❌ Brain/head graphics
- ❌ Literal "coaching" imagery (clipboards, whistles)
- ❌ Before/after weight loss style
- ❌ Meditation clichés (lotus position, closed eyes)

## 📸 Photography Brief (for Photographers)

### Project Overview
Create a library of lifestyle images showing real people having 
meaningful voice conversations. The mood is intimate, warm, and 
authentic—like Apple's "Shot on iPhone" campaign meets Patagonia's 
human-centered storytelling.

### Key Emotions to Capture
- Relief (finally being heard)
- Clarity (the moment of understanding)
- Connection (not alone)
- Hope (things can get better)
- Comfort (it's okay to ask for help)

### Technical Requirements
- RAW + processed JPGs
- Multiple aspect ratios (16:9, 4:5, 1:1)
- Warm color grade (reference: Kodak Portra 400)
- Natural/window light preferred
- Shallow depth of field for intimate feel

### Deliverables Needed
- 20 hero images (people on phone/computer)
- 10 detail shots (hands, coffee, environment)
- 5 environmental/lifestyle scenes
- Multiple crops of each for different platforms
