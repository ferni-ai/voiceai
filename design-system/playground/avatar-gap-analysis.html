🔊 Speaking: Three Layers Working Together

The web app uses three simultaneous animations to convey Ferni is speaking. The question is: which is the PRIMARY signal, and how do they work on iOS?

💫
1. Halo Pulse
The presence ring pulses outward like a speaker cone.

Web: Ring scales 0.6% with 0.45s rhythm
Role: Ambient indicator, peripheral vision
🫁
2. Body Pulse
Avatar squash/stretch with voice volume.

Web: Up to 12% scale, 0.4 squash ratio
Role: Primary speaking indicator
👄
3. Lid Mouth
Bottom lid opens/closes with volume.

Web: Y control 110→65 (max open)
Role: Speech articulation
Halo + Body
Lid Mouth
💡 Recommendation: Combine All Three
Primary signal: Body pulse (squash/stretch) - most visible, feels like breathing/speaking
Secondary: Halo pulse - peripheral awareness, "radiating" voice
Tertiary: Lid mouth - articulation detail, subtle but adds personality

The halo as speaker metaphor works - it's like sound waves emanating from the avatar. Combined with body pulse, it creates a convincing "speaking" effect without an actual mouth.
