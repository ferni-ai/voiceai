Pixar Animation Principles Applied

🎬 The Luxo Jr. Rule

The lamp shade creates expression, not the bulb. Our LIDS are the shade - they curve, they close in, they smile. The eyes (bulbs) stay CONSTANT.

⚖️ Squash & Stretch

Happy = body squashes down & widens. Surprised = stretches up & narrows. Always inverse relationship. This gives weight and life.

🎯 Anticipation

Before every big move, there's a small wind-up in the opposite direction. Happy bounces DOWN before settling. Surprised dips before stretching UP.

🌊 Follow-Through

Nothing stops instantly. Spring easing overshoots then settles. Eyes follow body movement with a slight delay (0.1s). Natural lag.

↗️ Secondary Action

The eyes drift after the body tilts. In "thinking", the body tilts FIRST, then eyes drift up-left 0.1s later. Overlapping action = life.

🎭 The Lids Are Everything

Happy = lids curve into smile. Sleepy = lids CLOSE IN (like heavy eyelids). Surprised = lids pull BACK. The lids tell the whole story.

Key insight: In "Sleepy", the lids close in from BOTH sides - the top comes way down, the bottom comes way up. The eyes stay the same white ellipses - but you see LESS of them because the lids are covering more. No pupils!
