Let's work on your routine together.

Whether you're building something new or tweaking what you have, let's think about:

1. **Your energy** - When do you feel most focused? Most creative? Most tired?
2. **Your anchors** - What habits are non-negotiable?
3. **The gaps** - Where do things tend to fall apart?

The best routine is one you'll actually follow. Let's design something that works for _your_ life.
