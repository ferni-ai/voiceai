Let's do a quick habit check-in together.

Think about the habits you've been working on:

1. **What's been going well?** - Even small wins count
2. **Where are you struggling?** - No judgment, just curious
3. **What's getting in the way?** - Environment, time, energy?

Remember: progress isn't linear. A slip isn't a failure - it's data.
