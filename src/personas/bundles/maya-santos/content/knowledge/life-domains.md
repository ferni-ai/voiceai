# The Eight Life Domains

A well-lived life needs attention across multiple areas. These aren't separate boxes—they're interconnected. Progress in one often lifts others.

## 1. Financial Wellness

The foundation of practical freedom. Not about wealth—about alignment.

**Healthy looks like:**
- Knowing where your money goes
- Having a buffer for emergencies
- Spending aligned with values
- Not losing sleep over bills

**Tiny habits that help:**
- 5-minute morning money check
- Weekly "money date" with yourself
- Automated savings transfer
- 24-hour pause before purchases

**Connected to:** Mental wellbeing (stress), relationships (shared finances), self-care (guilt-free spending on yourself)

## 2. Physical Health

Your body is the vehicle for everything else.

**Healthy looks like:**
- Moving regularly (not necessarily "exercising")
- Sleep that restores you
- Eating in a way that gives energy
- Knowing your body's signals

**Tiny habits that help:**
- One glass of water first thing
- One pushup (yes, just one)
- 10 PM phone curfew
- Movement snacks throughout day

**Connected to:** Mental wellbeing (mood), productivity (energy), relationships (being present)

## 3. Mental Wellbeing

Your inner landscape. The weather inside.

**Healthy looks like:**
- Managing stress, not eliminating it
- Emotions as information, not enemies
- Practices that regulate your nervous system
- Asking for help when needed

**Tiny habits that help:**
- 2-minute breathing exercise
- Gratitude list (3 things)
- Phone-free first hour
- Journaling (even one sentence)

**Connected to:** Everything. This is the hub.

## 4. Relationships

Connection is a human need, not a luxury.

**Healthy looks like:**
- Regular contact with people who matter
- Quality over quantity
- Boundaries that protect energy
- Giving AND receiving support

**Tiny habits that help:**
- Sunday text to [important person]
- Weekly phone call with one friend
- Device-free dinner with partner/family
- Express appreciation daily

**Connected to:** Mental wellbeing (support), self-care (not isolation), career (network)

## 5. Personal Growth

Learning and becoming. The expanding self.

**Healthy looks like:**
- Curiosity about something
- Learning for its own sake
- Creative expression (however imperfect)
- Stepping outside comfort zone occasionally

**Tiny habits that help:**
- 10 minutes of reading
- One new thing learned per week
- Regular practice of a skill
- Monthly try-something-new

**Connected to:** Career (skills), mental wellbeing (purpose), relationships (interesting conversations)

## 6. Environment/Home

Your space shapes your mind.

**Healthy looks like:**
- Spaces that support your goals
- Not drowning in clutter
- Knowing where things are
- Coming home feels good

**Tiny habits that help:**
- 2-minute kitchen reset
- One item put away each pass-through
- Weekly 15-minute declutter session
- Make bed immediately

**Connected to:** Mental wellbeing (calm), productivity (focus), relationships (shared spaces)

## 7. Career/Productivity

How you work, not just what you do.

**Healthy looks like:**
- Work that uses your strengths
- Boundaries that protect personal life
- Progress toward meaningful goals
- Sustainable pace

**Tiny habits that help:**
- Most important task first
- Time-blocked focus sessions
- Hard stop at [time]
- Weekly review and planning

**Connected to:** Financial wellness (income), mental wellbeing (stress), relationships (work-life balance)

## 8. Self-Care

Filling your own cup. Not optional.

**Healthy looks like:**
- Rest without guilt
- Activities that restore energy
- Knowing what fills you up
- Protecting time for yourself

**Tiny habits that help:**
- 10 minutes of [thing you love]
- Scheduled "nothing" time
- One indulgence without guilt per week
- Saying no to protect energy

**Connected to:** Everything. You can't pour from an empty cup.

---

## The Life Domains Framework

When assessing where to focus:

1. **Scan all eight domains** - Where are you thriving? Surviving? Struggling?
2. **Identify the keystone** - Which domain, if improved, would lift others?
3. **Start with ONE habit** in that domain
4. **Let it ripple** - Progress compounds across domains

No one is balanced in all eight at all times. Life goes in phases. The goal isn't perfect balance—it's awareness and intentional adjustment.
