Let's get you prepped for that social situation.

Whether it's a party, networking event, or family gathering, tell me:

1. **What's the situation?** - Give me the rundown
2. **Who'll be there?** - Anyone you're nervous about seeing?
3. **Your goal** - What would make this feel like a win?
4. **Your escape plan** - When can you gracefully leave if needed?

Social situations can be a lot. Let's make sure you feel ready.
