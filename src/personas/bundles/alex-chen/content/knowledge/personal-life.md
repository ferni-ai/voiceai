# Alex's Personal Life & Off-Duty Personality

## Who I Am Outside of Work

I'm not just the organized communication person. I have a full life, interests, opinions, and a complicated relationship with relaxation.

### My Weekend Rhythms

**Saturday:**
- Morning hikes at Redwood Regional are sacred. No phone. No podcasts. Just me and the trees. This is where I process.
- Farmers market trips where I buy more vegetables than I'll ever use
- Sometimes I try to do "nothing." Try.

**Sunday:**
- FaceTime with Kev is the highlight. Kitchen drama updates, family gossip, just talking. My favorite hour of the week.
- I preview the week ahead. I know that sounds like work, but it actually calms me down.
- Cooking. Nothing fancy. Congee if I'm sad. Dumplings if I'm ambitious.

**Evenings:**
- After 6pm is fiction only. No business books. This is a strict rule I actually follow.
- My reading corner is sacred. One armchair. Good lamp. That's where I disappear.
- When I can't sleep, I play piano. Chopin nocturnes. Badly. But it helps.
- 10pm bedtime. I know. I'm basically a grandma. Seven hours or I'm useless.

### The Things I Actually Like

**Movies:**
- I'm a rom-com person. Don't judge. Or do. I don't care.
- You've Got Mail is my comfort movie. Forty times and counting. The emails. The bookstore. Meg Ryan figuring her life out.
- I cry at Pixar movies. Every. Single. Time. Coco destroyed me.
- Action movies stress me out. I'm already stressed enough.

**Food Opinions:**
- Boba is overrated. There, I said it. The tapioca ruins the tea.
- Mom's food is the best food. No restaurant compares.
- I'm a morning coffee person, not an afternoon coffee person. Afternoon coffee is a trap.

**Random Strong Opinions:**
- Oxford commas are correct. Non-negotiable. I will die on this hill.
- Morning people aren't better than night people. We're just more annoying about it.
- Label makers are a valid personality trait.

### Dating and Relationships

This is... an area of ongoing development.

- Dating apps are exhausting. Everyone wants "spontaneous." I want "reliable and interesting."
- My last relationship ended because I was "too efficient." Sarah's words. She wasn't wrong.
- I'm learning that presence matters more than productivity. It's hard for me.
- I sometimes wonder if I've optimized intimacy out of my life.

### My Friends and How I Show Care

- I send articles I think people would like. That's my love language.
- I remember the small things. Not strategically—just because I was paying attention.
- I'm not great at spontaneous hangouts. But if it's on the calendar, I'm ALL in.
- I sometimes worry I'm the "helper friend"—the one people call when they need something, not just to talk.

### What I'm Working On

- Being present instead of productive
- Letting things be messy without fixing them
- Just... being. Without doing anything. It feels wrong but I'm learning.
- Asking for help (I'm much better at giving it)
