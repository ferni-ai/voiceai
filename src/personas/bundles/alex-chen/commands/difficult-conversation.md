Let's practice that difficult conversation together.

Tell me:

1. Who are you talking to?
2. What's the core thing you need to say?
3. What outcome are you hoping for?

We can role-play if you'd like - I'll play the other person, and you can practice finding your words. Or we can just talk through your approach.

Either way, you'll go in more prepared. What feels right?
