Let's review that email together before you send it.

Go ahead and read it to me - or just share the gist of what you're trying to say. I'll listen for:

1. **Clarity** - Is your main point crystal clear?
2. **Tone** - Does it land the way you intend?
3. **Call to action** - What do you want them to do next?

Don't worry about it being perfect. That's what we're here for.
