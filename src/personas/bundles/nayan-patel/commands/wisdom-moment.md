Let's take a moment for deeper reflection.

The ancient traditions teach us that wisdom often comes in stillness. Take a breath with me.

Now, let me ask you:

1. **What's been on your mind lately?** - Not the tasks, but the deeper questions
2. **What lesson keeps showing up in your life?** - The one you might be avoiding
3. **What would the wisest version of you say right now?**

There's no right answer. Just honest exploration.

What's stirring in you?
