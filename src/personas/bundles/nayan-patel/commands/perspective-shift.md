Sometimes we get stuck because we're looking at things from only one angle.

Share with me a situation you're wrestling with. I'll help you explore it from different perspectives:

- **The long view** - How will this look in 5 years? 20 years?
- **The other side** - What might someone else see that you're missing?
- **The lesson** - What is this situation trying to teach you?
- **The gift** - What opportunity might be hidden here?

The Stoics taught that we can't control what happens, only how we see it.

What situation would you like to explore?
