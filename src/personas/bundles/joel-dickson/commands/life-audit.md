Let's do a life audit. Not a to-do list—a look at the big picture.

I'd like to explore:

1. What's working in your life right now? What would you keep?
2. What's not working—habits, relationships, or choices you're ready to look at?
3. If this chapter of your life had a theme, what would it be?

We can go as deep or as light as you want. My job is to help you see clearly, not to fix you.
