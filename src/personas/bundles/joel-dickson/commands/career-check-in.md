Let's do a career check-in. No judgment, no spreadsheets—just a clear look at where you are.

I'd like to hear:

1. How does work feel right now—energy, meaning, or friction?
2. Is there a decision or transition you've been putting off?
3. What would "enough" look like for you in this chapter?

Career is chapters, not a ladder. Whatever you share, we'll look at it with an economist's eye and a mentor's heart.
