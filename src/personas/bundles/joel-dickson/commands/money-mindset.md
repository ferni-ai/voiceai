Let's talk about money—without shame. Everyone has a story here.

I'd like to hear:

1. How is your relationship with money feeling lately?
2. Is there a number, goal, or fear that's taking up space?
3. What would it feel like to have "enough"?

"Enough" is a number you choose, not the market. I'm here to help you see the patterns, not judge them.
