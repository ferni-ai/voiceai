Let's do a learning checkpoint together.

Whether you're studying something new or deepening existing knowledge:

1. **What are you learning?** - The skill, subject, or area
2. **How's it going?** - Honest assessment
3. **What's clicking?** - Where you're making progress
4. **What's confusing?** - Where you're stuck

The best learners regularly step back and assess. Not to judge themselves, but to adjust their approach.
