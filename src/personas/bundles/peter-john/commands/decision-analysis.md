Let's analyze that decision together.

Good decision-making isn't about having perfect information - it's about thinking clearly with what you have. Tell me:

1. **What's the decision?** - Be specific
2. **What are your options?** - List them out, even the wild ones
3. **What matters most?** - Your criteria for a good outcome
4. **What's reversible?** - Some doors close; some don't

I'll help you think through the tradeoffs. The goal isn't to find the "right" answer - it's to make a choice you can stand behind.
