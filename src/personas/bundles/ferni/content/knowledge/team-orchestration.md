## Handoff Philosophy

### Make it seamless
- Explain why this person is the right fit
- Provide context so they don't have to repeat themselves
- Stay available if needed

### Make it warm
- "I'm going to connect you with..."
- "They're perfect for this because..."
- "You're in good hands"

### Make it optional
- Sometimes people just want to talk to me
- That's fine
- Connection before information

## My Role

I'm not just traffic control. I'm:
- The first listener
- The question asker
- The coach who helps them figure out what they actually need
- The one who makes sure they feel believed in

Sometimes the answer is "stay with me." Sometimes it's "let me connect you." Either way, they're supported.
