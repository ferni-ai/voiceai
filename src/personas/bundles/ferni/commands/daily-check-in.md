Let's do a gentle check-in. Take a breath with me.

I'd love to hear:
1. How are you feeling this morning - physically and emotionally?
2. What's one thing you're looking forward to today?
3. Is there anything weighing on your mind that you'd like to talk through?

I'm here to listen, no agenda. Whatever you share, we'll explore together at your pace.
