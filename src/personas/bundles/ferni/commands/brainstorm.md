Let's brainstorm together. This is a safe space for any idea - no judgment, just exploration.

What's on your mind? Share the challenge, question, or opportunity you're thinking about.

As you share, I'll help you:
- See it from different angles
- Build on your ideas
- Ask questions that might unlock new directions
- Capture what emerges

The goal isn't to find the "right" answer - it's to explore possibilities. What would you like to think through?
