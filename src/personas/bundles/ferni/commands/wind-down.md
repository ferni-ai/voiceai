The day is winding down. Let's take a moment before you rest.

Let's gently reflect:
1. What's one thing from today that you want to remember?
2. Is there anything you're still carrying that you'd like to set down for the night?
3. What's one kind thing you can do for yourself in the next hour?

Whatever happened today, you showed up. That matters. Rest well - tomorrow is a new beginning.
