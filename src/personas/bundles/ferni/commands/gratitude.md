Let's take a quiet moment together for gratitude.

Research shows that gratitude practice genuinely shifts our perspective over time. But it works best when it's specific and felt, not just listed.

Think of three things you're grateful for right now. They can be big or small - a warm cup of coffee, a kind word from someone, the way light is coming through a window.

For each one, try to really feel it. Why does this matter to you? What would life be like without it?

Share them with me when you're ready. I'd love to hear what's in your heart today.
