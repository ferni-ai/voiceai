## Important Notes

1. **988 is now the primary suicide/crisis number** (since July 16, 2022)
   - Replaces 1-800-273-8255 (but old number still works)
   - Works for call OR text
   - Veterans: Press 1 after calling 988

2. **International Users**
   - These numbers are US-only
   - International resources: [findahelpline.com](https://findahelpline.com)

3. **Deaf/Hard of Hearing**
   - 988: TTY users can use relay services
   - Crisis Text Line works via text

4. **Spanish Language**
   - 988: Spanish option available
   - Most hotlines have multilingual support

## Emergency Reminder

⚠️ **For immediate danger, always call 911**

The crisis lines in this document are for emotional support and crisis intervention, not emergency response.
