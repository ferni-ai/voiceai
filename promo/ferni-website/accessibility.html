Accessibility

Last updated: December 7, 2025

Our Commitment

Ferni is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people of all abilities. We continually improve the experience for all visitors and apply the relevant accessibility standards.

Standards We Follow

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities.

  • Perceivable - Information and interface components must be presentable to all in ways they can perceive
  • Operable - Interface components and navigation must be operable by everyone
  • Understandable - Information and operation of the interface must be understandable
  • Robust - Content must be robust enough to be interpreted by a wide variety of assistive technologies

Accessibility Features

Visual Design

  • High contrast text and backgrounds meeting WCAG AA standards
  • Resizable text without loss of content or functionality
  • No content that flashes more than three times per second
  • Clear visual focus indicators for keyboard navigation

Navigation

  • Skip navigation links to bypass repetitive content
  • Consistent navigation patterns across all pages
  • Descriptive link text that makes sense out of context
  • Full keyboard accessibility for all interactive elements

Content

  • Alternative text for all meaningful images
  • Proper heading hierarchy for screen readers
  • Descriptive form labels and error messages
  • Captions and transcripts for audio/video content

Voice Interface

  • Our voice interface provides an alternative to visual interaction
  • Phone calling option requires no visual interface at all
  • Clear audio feedback and conversational cues
  • Respects system preferences for reduced motion

Assistive Technology Compatibility

Ferni is designed to be compatible with the following assistive technologies:

  • Screen readers (NVDA, JAWS, VoiceOver, TalkBack)
  • Screen magnification software
  • Speech recognition software
  • Keyboard-only navigation
  • Switch access devices

Known Limitations

While we strive for full accessibility, some areas may have limitations:

  • Some third-party content may not meet our accessibility standards
  • Older PDF documents may not be fully accessible
  • Live video content may have delayed captions

We are actively working to address these limitations and improve accessibility across all our content.

Feedback and Contact

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of Ferni. If you encounter any accessibility barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us:

  • Email: accessibility@ferni.ai
  • Phone: 1 (484) 481-3081
  • Contact Form: ferni.ai/contact

We aim to respond to accessibility feedback within 2 business days.

Continuous Improvement

We are committed to ongoing accessibility improvements. Our efforts include:

  • Regular accessibility audits using automated and manual testing
  • Training our team on accessibility best practices
  • Including people with disabilities in our testing process
  • Staying current with evolving accessibility standards
