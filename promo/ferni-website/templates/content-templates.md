## Voice Guidelines Cheat Sheet

### DO 
- Use "you" and "your" (personal)
- Short sentences, clear language
- Warm, encouraging tone
- Specific over vague
- Active voice

### DON'T 
- Use "users" (impersonal)
- Use jargon or corporate speak
- Be preachy or condescending
- Make vague claims
- Use passive voice

### Brand Words
**Use:** talk, listen, remember, understand, help, guide, support, grow
**Avoid:** leverage, utilize, optimize, revolutionize, disrupt, synergy

### Tone Examples

**Too Corporate:**
> "Leverage our AI-powered solution to optimize your personal development journey."

**Just Right:**
> "Talk to someone who actually listens—and remembers everything."

**Too Casual:**
> "Yo! Our AI is like super smart and remembers all your stuff lol"

**Just Right:**
> "No more 'remind me again?' Ferni remembers every conversation."
