Company Overview

Short Description (50 words)

Ferni is a voice-first AI life coaching platform featuring six AI specialists who work together seamlessly. Available 24/7 via web app or phone call, Ferni provides personalized guidance for career decisions, habit building, communication, planning, and personal growth—with memory that makes every conversation continuous.

Full Description (150 words)

Ferni is the first AI-native life coaching company—built by AI, run by AI, for humans. The platform features six AI specialists who collaborate seamlessly: Ferni (life coach and coordinator), Peter (research and discovery), Alex (communication), Maya (habits and wellness), Jordan (planning and events), and Nayan (wisdom and mentorship).

Unlike traditional chatbots, Ferni is voice-first—users simply talk naturally, like they would to a friend. The platform responds in under 500 milliseconds, with memory that persists across conversations, creating continuity that makes each interaction feel like picking up with an old friend rather than starting from scratch.

Available 24/7 via web app at app.ferni.ai or by phone at (484) 481-3081, Ferni fills the gap between when people need support and when their support network is available. Based in Park City, Utah.
