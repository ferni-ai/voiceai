# Product Hunt Launch Playbook

> **Goal**: #1 Product of the Day, 1,000+ upvotes, and 500+ sign-ups on launch day

---

## Executive Summary

Product Hunt is Ferni's best single-day growth opportunity because:
- Tech-savvy early adopters who love trying new AI tools
- Press/investors monitor daily rankings
- Permanent SEO benefit (PH pages rank well)
- Provides "social proof" badge for website

**Key insight**: Success on PH is 70% preparation, 30% execution day.


## Common Pitfalls to Avoid

### Don't Do This
- ❌ Ask for votes directly ("Please upvote!")
- ❌ Spam communities with vote requests
- ❌ Use bots or fake accounts
- ❌ Create new PH accounts to vote
- ❌ Vote from same IP address multiple times
- ❌ Disappear after launch (engagement matters)

### Do This
- ✅ Ask people to "check it out"
- ✅ Share in relevant communities where you're active
- ✅ Encourage honest feedback
- ✅ Respond to every comment
- ✅ Be authentic about your journey
- ✅ Thank people genuinely

## Hunter vs Self-Launch

### Using a Hunter (Recommended for first launch)

**Pros:**
- Their followers get notified
- Adds credibility
- They can post on your behalf

**How to Find Hunters:**
1. Look at recent successful AI product launches
2. Check hunter's follower count (aim for 1K+)
3. Reach out 2-3 weeks before launch
4. Offer them early access

**Outreach Template:**
```
Subject: Would you hunt our AI life coach on Product Hunt?

Hi [Name],

I noticed you hunted [similar product] and loved how you positioned it.

We're launching Ferni - an AI life coach with infinite memory. Unlike
ChatGPT or Replika, Ferni actually remembers your whole story and you
can even call it on the phone.

Would you be interested in hunting us? Happy to give you early access
to try it, and of course credit you on launch day.

Either way, appreciate what you do for the PH community!

[Name]
```

### Self-Launch

**When to self-launch:**
- You have 1K+ PH followers yourself
- Can't find a hunter who fits
- Want full control of timing/positioning

## Backup Plans

### If Launch Starts Slow
- Double down on comment engagement
- Ask inner circle to comment (not just vote)
- Share maker story on Twitter
- Cross-post to Reddit (carefully)

### If Technical Issues
- Have on-call engineer ready
- Create status page message
- Be transparent in PH comments
- Offer discount/extension for affected users

### If Negative Feedback
- Respond gracefully to every concern
- Don't get defensive
- Take valid criticism seriously
- Show you're listening
