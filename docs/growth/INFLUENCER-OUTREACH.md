# Influencer Outreach Playbook

> **Goal**: Partner with 50+ influencers in Year 1, driving 5,000+ sign-ups through authentic creator content

---

## Executive Summary

Influencer marketing works for Ferni because:
- AI coaching needs trust → creators provide social proof
- "I tried this for 30 days" content performs well
- Phone call feature is unique/shareable
- Self-improvement niche is massive and creator-heavy

---

## Influencer Tiers

### Tier 1: Nano (1K-10K followers)
**Investment**: Free product + small gift ($0-50)
**Expected ROI**: 50-200 sign-ups per creator
**Relationship**: Volume play, templated outreach

**Why Nano?**
- Higher engagement rates (5-10% vs 1-3% for larger)
- More likely to respond and be enthusiastic
- Can test messaging before scaling up
- Often create more authentic content

### Tier 2: Micro (10K-100K followers)
**Investment**: Free product + $100-500 per post
**Expected ROI**: 200-1,000 sign-ups per creator
**Relationship**: Personalized outreach, longer-term potential

**Why Micro?**
- Still relatable, not "too famous"
- Large enough to move the needle
- Often have highly engaged niche audiences
- More professional, easier to work with

### Tier 3: Mid-Tier (100K-500K followers)
**Investment**: $500-2,000 per post
**Expected ROI**: 1,000-5,000 sign-ups per creator
**Relationship**: Formal partnership, clear deliverables

**Why Mid-Tier?**
- Significant reach with still-engaged audience
- Can anchor a major campaign
- Often have management (easier logistics)
- Brand association value

### Tier 4: Macro (500K+ followers)
**Investment**: $2,000-10,000+ per post
**Expected ROI**: Variable, often brand awareness focused
**Relationship**: Agency negotiation, contracts

**When to Use Macro:**
- Major launch moments
- Brand awareness campaigns
- When you have proof from smaller tiers
- Specific strategic partnerships

## Outreach Workflow

### Step 1: Research (30 min/creator)

Before reaching out:
- [ ] Watch/read 5+ pieces of their content
- [ ] Note specific posts you can reference
- [ ] Understand their audience demographics
- [ ] Check if they've done similar partnerships
- [ ] Verify contact method (email vs DM)

### Step 2: First Touch

- [ ] Engage with 2-3 of their posts genuinely
- [ ] Follow/subscribe
- [ ] Wait 24-48 hours
- [ ] Send personalized outreach

### Step 3: Follow-Up Sequence

| Day | Action |
|-----|--------|
| 0 | Initial outreach |
| 3 | Like/comment on their content |
| 7 | Follow-up if no response |
| 14 | Final follow-up |
| 21+ | Move on, add to "try again later" list |

### Step 4: If They Respond

- [ ] Reply within 4 hours
- [ ] Answer questions directly
- [ ] Don't over-sell
- [ ] Get to next step (access, call, etc.)
- [ ] Send product access immediately

### Step 5: After They Try Product

| Day | Action |
|-----|--------|
| 1 | Send "how's it going?" check-in |
| 7 | Ask for feedback |
| 14 | Discuss potential content |
| 21+ | Formalize if interested |

## Content Guidelines for Creators

### What We Provide

**Asset Package**:
- High-res logo files
- Product screenshots
- Key talking points (not a script)
- Unique discount code
- UTM tracking link

**Talking Points** (Creators can choose what resonates):
- Infinite memory - Ferni remembers everything
- 6 AI personas for different needs
- You can actually call/text on the phone
- Available 24/7, especially at 2am
- Free tier is genuinely useful
- It's a coach, not a therapist

### What We Don't Dictate

- Exact script (authenticity matters)
- Specific phrases required
- Tone/style (match their usual content)
- Platform or format (their choice)

### Content Ideas to Suggest

**TikTok/Reels**:
- "I called an AI at 2am and this happened"
- "POV: Your AI coach remembers your ex's name"
- "Trying an AI life coach for a week"

**YouTube**:
- "I used an AI life coach for 30 days - here's what happened"
- "Best AI tools for personal development in 2026"
- "Replacing my therapist with AI for a week (my honest experience)"

**Instagram Stories**:
- Daily check-in series
- Live reaction to first call
- Poll: "Would you talk to an AI coach?"

### Disclosure Requirements

Require FTC-compliant disclosure:
- "#ad" or "#sponsored" visible
- Verbal disclosure in video ("Ferni partnered with me...")
- Clear and conspicuous (not hidden)

## Red Flags to Avoid

### Creator Red Flags

- Follower:engagement ratio way off (bought followers)
- Only posts sponsored content
- No response to DMs/email for 2+ weeks
- Requests full payment upfront
- Won't disclose as sponsored
- Generic content, no authentic voice
- History of controversy

### Deal Red Flags

- Asking for way above market rate
- Wanting to control all creative
- Demanding exclusivity without premium
- No interest in actually trying product
- Pushing for rush timeline

## Legal Considerations

### Contract Essentials

For any paid partnership:
- Deliverables (specific content + timeline)
- Compensation terms (amount + payment schedule)
- FTC disclosure requirements
- Content approval process (if any)
- Usage rights (can you repost?)
- Exclusivity period (if any)
- Termination clause

### FTC Compliance

Require creators to:
- Clearly disclose material relationship
- Use "#ad" or "#sponsored" visibly
- Verbal disclosure in video content
- Not make false claims about product
