## Video Production Workflow

### Tools Required
- **Recording**: Screen Studio ($89) or OBS (free)
- **Editing**: CapCut (free) - mobile optimized
- **Voiceover**: ElevenLabs or your voice
- **Captions**: CapCut auto-captions
- **Scheduling**: Later.com or Metricool

### Production Pipeline

```
Day 1: Batch record 20 screen captures of Ferni conversations
Day 2: Write 20 hooks and scripts
Day 3: Edit 10 videos (add captions, music, cuts)
Day 4: Edit 10 more videos
Day 5: Schedule all 20 videos across accounts
Day 6-7: Engage with comments, respond to DMs
Repeat weekly
```

### Video Specs
- **Aspect Ratio**: 9:16 (1080x1920)
- **Length**: 15-45 seconds optimal (under 60s)
- **Captions**: Always on, bold white with black outline
- **Music**: Trending sounds (check Creative Center)

### Quick Edit Checklist
- [ ] Hook in first 2 seconds
- [ ] Captions synced and readable
- [ ] Background music (lower volume than voice)
- [ ] No dead space (cut pauses)
- [ ] CTA in final 3 seconds
- [ ] Hashtags ready (3-5 max)

### Comment Seeding
Post first comment on every video:
- "What would you ask an AI life coach?"
- "Drop your biggest struggle and I'll tell you which persona to try"
- "Free tier = unlimited messages. No cap."

### Response Templates

**Positive Comment:**
```
"Omg thank you!! Have you tried it yet? LMK what you think"
```

**Skeptical Comment:**
```
"Totally get it - I was skeptical too. The free tier is unlimited so nothing to lose"
```

**Question About Features:**
```
"Yes! [Answer question]. The coolest part is [unique feature]. Link in bio to try"
```

**Competitor Mention:**
```
"I've tried that too! Main difference is Ferni actually remembers everything. Worth comparing"
```

### DM Strategy
When someone DMs with interest:

2. Ask what they're looking to work on
