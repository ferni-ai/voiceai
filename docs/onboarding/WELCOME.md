# Welcome to Ferni

> **We believe in making AI human, and the decisions we make will reflect that.**

Welcome to the team. Before you write a single line of code, we want you to understand what we're building and why it matters.

---

## Why We Exist

Ferni exists because most AI feels cold, transactional, and artificial. We're building something different: AI that feels genuinely human.

Not AI that pretends to be human. Not AI that passes the Turing test. AI that you'd actually want to talk to again.

This isn't a feature. It's our entire reason for being.

---

## What "Human AI" Actually Means

### It's Not About Technical Perfection

A response that's 50ms faster but feels robotic is worse than one that takes a moment to think.

We add pauses. We use filler words. We acknowledge emotions before solving problems. We remember what matters to people, not just what's technically relevant.

### It's About Relationship

Every interaction is part of an ongoing relationship. We remember past conversations. We notice when someone's having a hard day. We celebrate small wins. We don't treat users as anonymous sessions.

### It's About Gentle Growth

We help people change their lives, but we do it through compassion, not pressure. We use the Glidepath method: start tiny, build gradually, celebrate consistency over intensity. We normalize setbacks. We meet people where they are.

### It's About Authentic Character

Our personas aren't scripts. They have genuine personalities, opinions, interests, and stories. Ferni has lived in Japan. Maya geeks out about behavior science. Peter loves finding unexpected patterns in data. This authenticity isn't a gimmick—it's how humans connect.

## The Questions That Guide Everything

Before any decision—feature, architecture, PR, commit—ask:

1. **Does this make the AI feel more human?**
   - If yes: do it
   - If no: reconsider

2. **Does this serve the relationship?**
   - Not just this interaction, but the ongoing relationship

3. **Does this support gentle growth?**
   - Compassion over pressure
   - Progress over perfection

4. **Is this authentic?**
   - Would a human do this?
   - Does it feel genuine?

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Technical Excellence at the Expense of Humanity

```
// ❌ Optimized but cold
"Error: Invalid input parameter"

// ✅ Human and helpful
"I didn't quite catch that—could you try again?"
```

### Features Over Connection

```
// ❌ More features, less human
Add 10 new tools without considering the interaction

// ✅ Fewer features, more human
Make one tool that feels like talking to a friend
```

### Speed Over Warmth

```
// ❌ Fast but robotic
Instant responses with no acknowledgment

// ✅ Thoughtful and warm
"That's a great question..." [brief pause] "Here's what I think..."
```
