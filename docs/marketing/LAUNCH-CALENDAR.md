## Platform-Specific Timing

### Twitter/X
- Best: Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM PT
- Post frequency: 2-3x per day launch week, then 1x daily
- Threads perform best at 9 AM PT

### LinkedIn
- Best: Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM PT
- Post frequency: 1x per day launch week, then 2-3x per week
- Avoid weekends

### Product Hunt
- Launch at 12:01 AM PT
- Most activity: First 6 hours
- Respond within 30 minutes

### Hacker News
- Best: Tuesday-Thursday, 6-8 AM PT
- Don't ask for upvotes (bannable)
- Be in comments within first hour

### Dev.to / Hashnode
- Best: Tuesday-Wednesday morning
- Cross-post with 24-hour delay
- Engage in comments

### Reddit
- Best: Tuesday-Thursday, 10 AM PT
- Follow subreddit rules strictly
- 10:1 ratio (10 comments for every post)

## Emergency Playbook

### If Product Hunt underperforms
- [ ] Double down on direct outreach
- [ ] Pivot energy to Hacker News
- [ ] More Twitter engagement
- [ ] Consider relaunch in 2 weeks

### If site goes down
- [ ] Status page update within 5 minutes
- [ ] Twitter acknowledgment
- [ ] ETA for fix
- [ ] Post-mortem within 24 hours

### If negative feedback
- [ ] Acknowledge publicly
- [ ] Don't be defensive
- [ ] Fix if valid, explain if not
- [ ] Follow up with resolution

### If competitor launches same day
- [ ] Don't mention them
- [ ] Focus on unique value
- [ ] Stay positive
- [ ] Maybe delay to next week
