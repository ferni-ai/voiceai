## Engagement Strategy

### Pre-launch (1 week)
- Tease with "working on something" posts
- Build anticipation without revealing
- Save potential RT-ers for launch day

### Launch Day
1. Post thread at 9 AM PT (peak engagement)
2. Reply to your own thread with GIFs/videos
3. Engage with EVERY reply for first 2 hours
4. Quote RT the coolest responses
5. Cross-post to LinkedIn

### Post-launch
- Share user creations (with permission)
- Post tips and tricks
- Behind-the-scenes technical content
- Respond to DMs promptly
