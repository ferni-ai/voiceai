## 🎨 Style Foundation (Apply to ALL prompts)

### Base Style Suffix

Add this to the end of every prompt:

```
Flat vector illustration, organic flowing shapes, earthy color palette
(sage green #4a6741, warm cream #F5F1E8, terracotta #a67a6a,
natural brown #2C2520, warm amber #C4A265), smooth gradients,
no outlines, minimalist, Apple-style, Notion-style illustrations,
abstract human forms, NOT sharp edges, NOT tech/circuit aesthetic,
NOT blue tones, NOT corporate clip art
```

### Color Palette

| Color       | Hex       | Use                             |
| ----------- | --------- | ------------------------------- |
| Ferni Sage  | `#4a6741` | Primary accent, nature elements |
| Paper Cream | `#F5F1E8` | Backgrounds, negative space     |
| Terracotta  | `#a67a6a` | Warm accents, human elements    |
| Natural Ink | `#2C2520` | Grounding elements              |
| Warm Amber  | `#C4A265` | Highlights, light sources       |
| Soft Coral  | `#c4856a` | Warmth, energy                  |

### Style References

- Notion illustrations
- Apple Human Interface Guidelines
- Headspace app illustrations
- Slack loading illustrations
- Abstract, organic, flowing

## ❌ What to Avoid in ALL Prompts

- Blue or cool tones
- Sharp geometric edges
- Circuit board / tech aesthetic
- Corporate clip art style
- Outlined illustrations
- Neon or bright saturated colors
- Literal AI/robot imagery
- Complex busy compositions
- Cold or sterile feeling
- Stock illustration aesthetic

---

## ✅ What to Include in ALL Prompts

- Organic flowing shapes
- Earthy color palette (sage, terracotta, amber, cream)
- Warm gradients
- Minimalist composition
- Human-centered feeling (even if abstract)
- Lots of negative space
- Soft, inviting energy
- Movement and flow suggestion
- Apple/Notion illustration reference
