# 🎵 Ferni Sound Design Brief

> Creating delightful, warm audio moments for the Ferni AI life coach experience.

## Brand Audio Identity

**Core Feeling:** Like being welcomed by a wise friend into a cozy, safe space.

**Audio Palette:**

- **Warm** - Not cold or clinical
- **Organic** - Real instruments, natural sounds
- **Earthy** - Matches our sage green/brown visual palette
- **Human** - Feels like someone is there, not a machine
- **Delightful** - Small moments of joy, like a smile in sound

**Avoid:**

- ❌ Robotic beeps or synthetic bloops
- ❌ Harsh, startling sounds
- ❌ Generic "tech" notification sounds
- ❌ Over-the-top cinematic drama
- ❌ Anything cold, clinical, or corporate

---

## Sound Files Needed

### 1. `dramatic-entrance.mp3` — First Meeting with a Specialist

**Duration:** 1.0 - 1.5 seconds

**When it plays:** User meets a specialist persona (Peter, Alex, Maya, Jordan, Nayan) for the FIRST time. This is a special moment - like being introduced to someone who's going to help you.

**The Feeling:** "Someone special just arrived to help you." A gentle reveal, warm anticipation, quiet excitement.

**Sound Description:**

- Soft orchestral swell that blooms open (strings or warm brass)
- OR gentle piano chord that opens up with subtle shimmer
- OR acoustic guitar strum with harmonic overtone
- Ends with a gentle "landing" - feels complete, not cut off
- Should feel like a curtain gently parting

**Reference Sounds:**

- [Pixabay: Glorious Reveal Orchestral Sting](https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/glorious-reveal-orchestral-sting-426258/) - but WARMER, less cinematic
- [Pixabay: Warm Piano Logo](https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/warm-piano-logo-116098/) - this warmth, but shorter

**AI Prompt:**

```
Create a 1.2-second warm, gentle "reveal" sound effect.
Soft strings or piano that swells open like a flower blooming.
Warm, organic, inviting - like meeting someone special.
End with a gentle resolution. No harsh attack.
Think "wise friend arriving" not "superhero entrance."
Key: C major or G major. Tempo: 80-90 BPM feel.
```

---

### 2. `connect.mp3` — Session Start (Ferni Greeting)

**Duration:** 0.5 - 0.8 seconds

**When it plays:** User connects to start a session with Ferni (the main coach).

**The Feeling:** "Hey, I'm here." Warm acknowledgment, like a friend picking up the phone.

**Sound Description:**

- Soft, warm tone - like a gentle "hello" in sound form
- Single note or simple two-note motif that rises slightly
- Could be: soft mallet on wood, gentle pluck, warm synth pad with attack
- Feels grounded and present

**Reference Sounds:**

- [Freesound: Atmospheric Notification](https://freesound.org/people/original_sound/sounds/494979/) - this vibe but shorter
- [Pixabay: Notification Piano](https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/notification-piano-443094/) - this warmth

**AI Prompt:**

```
Create a 0.6-second warm "connection" notification sound.
Soft, organic tone - like a gentle wooden mallet or warm piano note.
Rises slightly in pitch, feels welcoming and grounded.
Think "friend picking up the phone" not "app notification."
Warm, earthy, human.
```

---

### 3. `disconnect.mp3` — Session End

**Duration:** 0.5 - 0.8 seconds

**When it plays:** Session ends, user disconnects.

**The Feeling:** "See you soon." Gentle closure, like a warm hug goodbye.

**Sound Description:**

- Mirror of connect.mp3 but descends slightly
- Feels like a gentle "putting down" or "resting"
- Should feel complete and satisfying, not abrupt
- Could fade out softly

**AI Prompt:**

```
Create a 0.7-second warm "goodbye" notification sound.
Gentle descent, feels like setting something down softly.
Mirror the "connect" sound but resolving downward.
Warm, satisfying closure - like a friend saying "talk soon."
Slight reverb tail that fades naturally.
```

## Persona Handoff Sounds

Each specialist has a unique handoff sound that plays when Ferni hands off to them. These should be **short (0.6-1.0s)**, **distinctive**, and **match the persona's energy**.

### 4. `handoff-to-ferni.mp3` — Return to Coach

**Persona:** Ferni (Life Coach) - Warm, grounded, wise

**The Feeling:** Coming home. Back to your anchor.

**Sound Description:**

- Warm, grounding - like sitting by a fireplace
- Soft acoustic guitar chord OR warm piano
- Feels safe and familiar

**AI Prompt:**

```
Create a 0.8-second warm "coming home" sound.
Soft acoustic guitar chord or gentle piano - C major.
Grounding, safe, familiar - like returning to a wise friend.
Earthy, organic, no synthetic elements.
```

---

### 5. `handoff-to-peter.mp3` — Research Specialist

**Persona:** Peter John - Intellectual, curious, analytical, thorough

**The Feeling:** "Let's figure this out together." Curious, thoughtful.

**Sound Description:**

- Gentle bells or xylophone/marimba notes
- Ascending pattern (curiosity, exploration)
- Light, clear, but warm - not cold or clinical
- Think: "opening a book" in sound form

**AI Prompt:**

```
Create a 0.8-second "curious discovery" sound.
Gentle bells or xylophone - 2-3 ascending notes.
Intellectual but warm - like opening an interesting book.
Clear and light, but not cold. Key of D major.
Think "thoughtful researcher" not "laboratory."
```

---

### 6. `handoff-to-alex.mp3` — Communications Specialist

**Persona:** Alex Chen - Dynamic, articulate, energetic, social

**The Feeling:** "Let's make this happen!" Energetic, confident, warm.

**Sound Description:**

- Upbeat string pizzicato OR bright acoustic strum
- Slightly faster, more energetic than others
- Confident but approachable
- Think: ready to take action

**AI Prompt:**

```
Create a 0.8-second "let's do this" energetic sound.
Upbeat string pizzicato or bright guitar strum.
Confident, dynamic, warm - ready to communicate and act.
Faster tempo feel than other sounds. Key of A major.
Think "enthusiastic friend ready to help" not "corporate presentation."
```

---

### 7. `handoff-to-maya.mp3` — Habits & Routines Specialist

**Persona:** Maya Santos - Steady, rhythmic, consistent, nurturing

**The Feeling:** "One step at a time." Grounded, rhythmic, supportive.

**Sound Description:**

- Soft marimba or gentle percussion
- Steady, rhythmic quality (habits = rhythm)
- Warm wood tones
- Think: a gentle heartbeat, consistent and reliable

**AI Prompt:**

```
Create a 0.8-second "steady rhythm" sound.
Soft marimba or wood percussion - warm, organic.
Has a rhythmic quality - like a gentle heartbeat.
Grounded, consistent, nurturing - supports good habits.
Think "supportive routine" not "alarm clock."
```

---

### 8. `handoff-to-jordan.mp3` — Events & Life's Firsts Specialist

**Persona:** Jordan Taylor - Exciting, celebratory, adventure-loving

**The Feeling:** "Something exciting is happening!" Bright, celebratory.

**Sound Description:**

- Bright, uplifting - hint of fanfare without being cheesy
- Could include subtle sparkle or shimmer
- More energetic/excited than other sounds
- Think: the moment before opening a gift

**AI Prompt:**

```
Create a 0.9-second "celebration ahead" sound.
Bright, uplifting - subtle brass fanfare with shimmer.
Exciting but not over-the-top. Anticipation of joy.
Like the moment before opening a gift or starting an adventure.
Key of F major. Think "party planner arrives" not "game show."
```

---

### 9. `handoff-to-nayan.mp3` — Wisdom & Philosophy Specialist

**Persona:** Nayan Patel - Serene, wise, contemplative, deep

**The Feeling:** "Let's go deeper." Peaceful, wise, expansive.

**Sound Description:**

- Singing bowl OR gentle wind chimes
- Resonant, with natural decay/sustain
- Feels spacious and timeless
- Think: entering a peaceful temple

**AI Prompt:**

```
Create a 1.0-second "wisdom arriving" sound.
Singing bowl strike or deep, resonant chime.
Peaceful, wise, expansive - invites contemplation.
Let the sound ring and decay naturally.
Think "entering a peaceful sanctuary" not "spa music."
```

## Technical Specifications

| Parameter       | Requirement                        |
| --------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| **Format**      | MP3, 128-192 kbps                  |
| **Sample Rate** | 44.1 kHz                           |
| **Channels**    | Stereo preferred                   |
| **Duration**    | 0.5s - 1.5s (see individual specs) |
| **Loudness**    | -16 to -14 LUFS (podcast standard) |
| **Headroom**    | -1 dB true peak                    |
| **Fade Out**    | Natural decay or 50-100ms fade     |
